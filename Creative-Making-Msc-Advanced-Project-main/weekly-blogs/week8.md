# Week 8
# Sloth

### Research 

Sloth represents heaviness, delay, and resistance to action. I researched time-based visual systems and delay feedback techniques. Instead of making the performer move slowly, I wanted the system itself to feel slow, as if it were dragging the body into stillness.

---

### Development

While developing Sloth, I introduced long feedback decay and delayed motion response. Movements did not appear immediately on screen but slowly dissolved into the image. Heavy blur and soft edges made everything feel thick and resistant. When I moved quickly, the system almost ignored me. Only slow movements were fully visualised, which naturally forced me to slow down.

### Reflection

Sloth created one of the strongest bodily effects on me as a performer. I did not consciously decide to move slower — the system made me do so. This made me realise again that emotional behaviour in this project is not only visual but also physical.
