# Week 9
# Greed

### Research 

Greed is strongly connected to accumulation, possession, and excess over time. I wanted to move away from immediate reaction and explore how emotional pressure can build gradually. I researched persistence-based feedback systems and long-term visual accumulation.

---

### Development

Greed was built around the idea of continuous gathering. No matter how I moved, the system kept adding more visual matter to the screen. Nothing was ever fully cleared. The silhouette attracted visual elements that stayed and piled up over time. As the interaction continued, the screen became denser and heavier, even if my movements stayed minimal.

### Reflection

Greed showed me that time itself can become an emotional driver. The emotional weight of this system does not come from violent reaction but from the fact that it never lets go. This helped me expand my understanding of how procedural systems can express desire and accumulation.
