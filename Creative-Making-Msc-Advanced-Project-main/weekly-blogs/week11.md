# Week 11
# Lust

### Research 

Lust required a very different emotional direction from all previous sins. I researched softness, sensual movement, and colour-based emotional perception. I wanted to avoid obvious sexual imagery and instead focus on atmosphere, intimacy, and ambiguity.

---

### Development

Lust became the softest system in the entire project. I used translucent layers, slow feedback cycles, and gentle colour gradients. The silhouette gradually blended into the background rather than standing out sharply. Movement became smooth and pulsating. Compared with Wrath or Gluttony, this system felt quiet, fragile, and intimate.

### Reflection

Designing Lust made me realise that not all emotions need strong visual contrast to be felt. Softness, blending, and ambiguity can be just as powerful as aggression or excess. It also completed the emotional spectrum of the Seven Sins in a more balanced way.
