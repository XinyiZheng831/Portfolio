# Week 10
# Gluttony

### Research 

Gluttony is related to overconsumption, swelling, and bodily excess. I researched fluid distortion, expansion-based deformation, and the visual language of overflow. I wanted this system to feel physically “too much”.

---

### Development

In Gluttony, the silhouette was pushed outward into bloated, fluid forms. The body appeared to swell, stretch, and overflow the screen. Feedback layers built up quickly, and boundaries between the body and the background became unclear. The system felt heavy, thick, and excessive, as if it were constantly consuming more than it could hold.

### Reflection

Compared to Greed, which built up slowly, Gluttony felt immediate and overwhelming. This contrast helped clarify the emotional differences between accumulation and excess. It also made me pay more attention to material qualities such as thickness, softness, and pressure.
