### Reading & Reflections

Reading:

The exploration of the sense of embodiment in VR deepened my understanding of how VR reshapes not just visual experience but self-perception. Morie’s article comprehensively reviews body ownership, agency, and self-location, highlighting that “presence” is central in immersive environments. It made me reflect on what kind of new existence digital worlds offer, and how this might even affect our identity in the real world.

Working:

I began thinking more deeply about how narrative and environment co-evolve. A compelling visual atmosphere is not just about aesthetic appeal—it needs to reflect narrative shifts and emotional beats. Working on the main scene helped me realize how important “timing” and “motion cues” are for guiding the player’s experience.
